Vegetable Stir-Fry

Description

Colorful, crisp, and bursting with flavor, Vegetable Stir-Fry is a vibrant dish that brings fresh veggies to life in just minutes. Featuring a medley of seasonal vegetables like bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, and snap peas, this quick-cooked meal is tossed in a savory stir-fry sauce made with soy sauce, garlic, and ginger. It's a healthy, plant-based favorite that’s perfect on its own or served over rice or noodles — ideal for busy weeknights, meal prep, or anyone craving a light yet satisfying dish.

Ingredients

  • Broccoli
  • Bell peppers
  • Carrots
  • Snow peas
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Ginger

Steps to make Vegetable stir fry

  1. Prepare the Vegetables

    2. Wash and chop all vegetables into bite-sized pieces.

  2. Heat the Pan

    3. In a large wok or skillet, heat sesame oil over medium-high heat. Add minced garlic and grated ginger. Sauté until fragrant.

  3. Stir-Fry

    4. Add the firmer vegetables first (like carrots and broccoli), followed by softer ones (like bell peppers and snow peas). Stir constantly for even cooking.

  4. Add the Sauce

    5. Drizzle soy sauce over the vegetables. Stir and cook for a few more minutes until veggies are crisp-tender.

  5. Finish and Serve

    6. Top with sliced green onions and serve immediately with rice or noodles.
