Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Description

Creamy, comforting, and packed with flavor, Chicken Alfredo Pasta is a classic Italian-American dish that never goes out of style. Tender slices of seasoned chicken breast are served over a bed of fettuccine noodles, all coated in a rich, velvety Alfredo sauce made with butter, garlic, cream, and freshly grated parmesan cheese. It's a satisfying, restaurant-quality meal that's surprisingly simple to make at home — perfect for weeknight dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Chicken breast
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Heavy cream
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Parsley

Steps to make Chicken Alfredo

  1. Cook the Pasta

    2. Boil fettuccine in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  2. Prepare the Chicken

    3. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper. In a large skillet, cook them in a bit of olive oil until golden and cooked through. Remove and slice.

  3. Make the Alfredo Sauce

    4. In the same skillet, melt butter and sauté minced garlic. Pour in the heavy cream, stirring gently. Add grated parmesan and stir until smooth and creamy.

  4. Combine

    5. Add the cooked pasta and sliced chicken to the sauce. Toss to coat evenly.

  5. Garnish and Serve

    6. Sprinkle with freshly chopped parsley and more parmesan before serving.
