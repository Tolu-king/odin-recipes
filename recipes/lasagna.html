Lasagna

description

A classic homemade lasagna layered with rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella — the perfect comfort food for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Egg
  • Olive oil
  • Herbs and spices (basil, oregano, parsley, salt, pepper)

Steps to make lasagna

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce

    2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil and sauté chopped onions and garlic until fragrant. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and herbs (basil, oregano, salt, and pepper). Simmer for 20–30 minutes to allow flavors to meld.

  2. Cook the Noodles

    3. Boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside on a clean surface to prevent sticking.

  3. Mix the Ricotta Filling

    4. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese with an egg, chopped parsley, salt, and pepper. Stir until smooth and creamy.

  4. Assemble the Lasagna

    5. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, followed by a layer of noodles, then a layer of ricotta mixture. Sprinkle with mozzarella and a bit of parmesan. Repeat the layers (sauce, noodles, ricotta, cheese) until all ingredients are used, finishing with a generous layer of cheese on top.

  5. Bake

    6. Cover with foil and bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for about 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 20–25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and slightly golden.

  6. Rest and Serve

    7. Let the lasagna rest for 10–15 minutes before slicing. This helps it set and makes serving easier.
